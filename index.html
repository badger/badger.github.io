Badger.com has shutdown.

On June 27th, 2016, Badger.com canceled its ICANN accreditation, and all domains were transferred to Namecheap.com.

If you are already a Namecheap customer, please login to your account and check "Domain List"; your domain(s) formerly with Badger.com should be added there. For those who didn't previously have a Namecheap account, a new account was created using the registrant email address listed in the domain's public Whois information. To get your login credentials, please follow the instructions in this article.If you have any questions about your domains, please feel free to contact Namecheap's customer support at https://www.namecheap.com/support.aspx.
